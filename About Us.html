Myth or Fact?

Think you know your food facts? Tap to reveal the truth!

Popcorn is unhealthy

Myth! Air-popped popcorn is actually a whole grain and can be a healthy snack. It's low in calories and high in fiber. The unhealthy part comes from added butter, salt, and oil.

All fats are bad

Myth! Not all fats are bad. Healthy fats found in avocados, nuts, and fish are essential for your body. They support brain function, hormone production, and help absorb vitamins.

Chocolate makes you gain weight

Partly True! Dark chocolate in moderation can actually be part of a healthy diet. It contains antioxidants and can improve heart health. However, milk chocolate with added sugar can contribute to weight gain.

Carbs will always make you fat

Myth! Carbohydrates are your body's main energy source. Complex carbs like whole grains, fruits, and vegetables provide essential nutrients. It's the portion size and type of carbs that matter.
